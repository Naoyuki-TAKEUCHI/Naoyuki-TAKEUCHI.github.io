Short biography

Naoyuki Takeuchi is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Geophysics, Graduate School of Science, Kyoto University. His research focuses on the Martian nightside ionosphere, auroral electron precipitation, and ion composition using Mars Express and MAVEN observations.
